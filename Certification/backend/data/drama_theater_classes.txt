Drama and Theater Classes
The Drama and Theater Classes is organized by Spotlight Youth Theater. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 7-18 age range.

- Fall Session: September 9, 2024 - November 8, 2024
- Winter Session: January 6, 2025 - March 7, 2025
- Spring Session: March 17, 2025 - May 16, 2025
- Summer Intensive: June 9, 2025 - August 1, 2025

Class Types:

Acting Fundamentals (Ages 7-10):
- Tuesday & Thursday, 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM
- Focus: Character development, improvisation, scene work

Intermediate Acting (Ages 11-14):
- Monday & Wednesday, 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM
- Focus: Script analysis, audition techniques, ensemble work

Advanced Theater (Ages 15-18):
- Tuesday & Thursday, 6:30 PM - 8:30 PM
- Focus: Shakespeare, contemporary drama, audition prep

Musical Theater (All ages):
- Saturday, 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Focus: Acting, singing, choreography integration

Technical Theater (Ages 12-18):
- Wednesday, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Focus: Lighting, sound, set design, stage management
