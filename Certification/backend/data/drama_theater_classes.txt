Drama and Theater Classes
The Drama and Theater Classes is organized by Spotlight Youth Theater. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 7-18 age range.

Program Features:
This program offers several valuable features including Professional theater instructors, Performance opportunities, Costume and prop design, Technical theater training, Showcase performances, and Audition preparation.

Pricing Information:
- 8 Week Session: $299
- Production Participation: $350 (includes costume)
- Annual Membership: $950 (unlimited classes)
