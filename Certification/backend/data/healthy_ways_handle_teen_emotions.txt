Healthy Ways to Handle Teen Emotions
The Healthy Ways to Handle Teen Emotions is organized by Community Health Initiative. This program falls under the Community category.

Workshop Series Dates:
- Session 1: January 18, 2025 - Understanding Teen Brain Development
- Session 2: February 15, 2025 - Managing Stress and Anxiety
- Session 3: March 22, 2025 - Communication Skills for Teens
- Session 4: April 19, 2025 - Building Resilience
- Session 5: May 17, 2025 - Mindfulness and Self-Care

Time: All workshops 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
Location: Round Rock Community Center, Conference Room B
Target Audience: Parents, teens (ages 13-18), and educators

Workshop Topics:

Understanding Teen Emotions:
- Adolescent brain development
- Hormonal changes and mood
- Normal vs. concerning behaviors
- When to seek professional help

Coping Strategies:
- Stress management techniques
- Healthy outlets for emotions
- Mindfulness and meditation
- Physical activity and sleep hygiene
- Creative expression

Communication Skills:
- Active listening
- Expressing feelings effectively
- Conflict resolution
- Building trust with teens
- Setting healthy boundaries

Mental Health Resources:
- Local counseling services
- Crisis hotlines
- School support systems
- Online resources and apps
- Support groups

Warning Signs:
- Depression and anxiety indicators
- Substance abuse risk factors
- Self-harm behaviors
- Eating disorders
- Suicidal ideation

Workshop Format:
- Expert presentations
- Interactive discussions
- Role-playing exercises
- Q&A sessions
- Take-home resource packets

Presenters:
- Licensed therapists
- School counselors
- Teen psychologists
- Youth mentors

Cost:
- Free for all participants
- Materials provided
- Refreshments included
