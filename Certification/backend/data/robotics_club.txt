Robotics Club
The Robotics Club is organized by Tech Innovators Youth Program. This program falls under the School category. It is designed for participants in the Grades 6-12 age range.

Season: 2024-2025
Meeting Schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 3:30-5:30 PM
Additional Saturday sessions during competition season
Location: STEM Lab, Building C

Program Structure:

Middle School Team (Grades 6-8):
- VEX IQ Competition
- Focus on fundamentals
- Team building activities
- Local competitions

High School Team (Grades 9-12):
- VEX Robotics Competition (VRC)
- Advanced programming and design
- Regional and state competitions
- Potential world championship qualification

Season Timeline:
- September 2024: Robot reveal and strategy planning
- September-November: Build season
- December 2024-February 2025: Competition season
- March-April 2025: Championship events
- May 2025: VEX Worlds (if qualified)

Competition Schedule 2024-2025:
- Qualifier 1: November 16, 2024

- Qualifier 3: January 18, 2025
- Regional Championship: February 22, 2025
- State Championship: March 15, 2025
- VEX Worlds: April 23-26, 2025 (Dallas, TX)
