Chess Club for Kids
The Chess Club for Kids is organized by Strategic Minds Chess Academy. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 6-16 age range.

Skill Levels Offered:

Beginner Level:
Learn the basics of chess Topics covered include: Piece movements, Basic strategies, Checkmate patterns.

Intermediate Level:
Improve your game Topics covered include: Opening principles, Middle game tactics, Endgame techniques.

Advanced Level:
Competitive preparation Topics covered include: Advanced strategies, Tournament play, Chess analysis.

Meeting Schedule:
- Beginner: Saturdays, 10:00-11:00 AM
- Intermediate: Saturdays, 11:00 AM-12:00 PM
- Advanced: Saturdays, 1:00-2:30 PM

Program Features:
This program offers several valuable features including USCF certified instructors, Weekly lessons and practice, Tournament opportunities, Chess set provided, Online practice platform, and Monthly club tournaments.

Benefits:
Participants will gain numerous benefits such as Improves critical thinking, Enhances concentration, Develops problem-solving skills, Builds patience and sportsmanship, and Makes new friends.
