Baseball League
The Baseball League is organized by Austin Youth Baseball Association. This program falls under the Community category.

Season Schedule:
- Spring Season: March 1 - June 15, 2025
- Fall Season: September 1 - November 30, 2025
- Practice starts two weeks before first game

Age Divisions:
- T-Ball: Ages 4-6
- Coach Pitch: Ages 7-8
- Minor League: Ages 9-10
- Major League: Ages 11-12

Program Features:
This program offers several valuable features including Certified coaches, Full uniforms, Umpires for games, End of season tournament, and All-star teams.

Registration Fees:
- Early Bird (by January 15, 2025): $150
- Regular (by February 1, 2025): $175
- Late Registration: $200

Registration Information:
