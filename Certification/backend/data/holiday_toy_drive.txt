Holiday Toy Drive

The Holiday Toy Drive is organized by Community Care Coalition. This is a Charitable Donation Drive. Category: Community.

Collection Period: November 1 - December 15, 2024
Distribution Day: December 20, 2024

Drop-off Locations:
- Round Rock ISD schools (all campuses)
- Cedar Park Public Library
- Round Rock Community Center
- Local fire stations
- Participating retail stores

Toy Distribution Event:
- Date: December 20, 2024
- Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Location: Community Care Coalition Center
- Address: 1500 Opportunity Drive, Round Rock, TX 78681

Needed Items:
- New, unwrapped toys
- Age range: Infants to teens
- Educational toys and games
- Sports equipment
- Art supplies
- Books
- Gift cards for teens

Most Needed:
- Toys for ages 10-17
- Gender-neutral options
- STEM toys
- Board games
- Books for all ages

How to Help:

Donate Toys:
- Drop off at any collection location
- Host a toy drive at your workplace
- Purchase from wish list on website
- Monetary donations accepted

Volunteer Opportunities:
- Collection site monitors (November-December)
- Toy sorting: December 16-18, 2024, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Distribution day: December 20, 2024, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Wrapping station helpers

2023 Impact:
- 5,000+ toys collected
- 1,500 families served
- 3,000+ children received gifts
