Low Cost Counseling Services
The Low Cost Counseling Services is organized by Community Access Center (CAC). This program falls under the Community category.

Services Available: Year-round, ongoing enrollment

Office Hours:
- Monday-Thursday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed
- Same-day appointments available for crisis situations

Location:
Community Access Center
1200 Wellness Way, Round Rock, TX 78664

Services Offered:

Individual Therapy:
- Depression and anxiety
- Stress management
- Grief and loss
- Life transitions
- Self-esteem issues
- Trauma and PTSD

Family Therapy:
- Parent-child conflicts
- Blended family issues
- Communication problems
- Divorce adjustment
- Family dynamics

Couples Counseling:
- Relationship issues
- Communication skills
- Pre-marital counseling
- Trust and intimacy
- Conflict resolution

Specialized Services:
- Adolescent counseling (ages 12-18)
- Substance abuse support
- Anger management
- Eating disorder support
- LGBTQ+ affirmative therapy
- Bilingual services (English/Spanish)

Therapist Qualifications:
- Licensed Professional Counselors (LPC)
- Licensed Clinical Social Workers (LCSW)
- Licensed Marriage and Family Therapists (LMFT)
- Supervised interns available at reduced rates

How It Works:
1. Call for intake appointment
2. Complete initial assessment
3. Discuss goals and treatment plan
4. Schedule ongoing sessions
5. Payment determined by sliding scale

Pricing Information:
- Model: Sliding scale based on household income
- Starting At: $20 per session
- Standard Rate: $60-$120 per session
- Insurance: Some insurance accepted (Medicaid, CHIP, select private plans)
- Payment Options: Cash, Check, Credit card, Payment plans available
- No one turned away for inability to pay

Sliding Scale Examples:
- Income under $20,000: $20 per session
- Income $20,000-$40,000: $40 per session
- Income $40,000-$60,000: $60 per session
- Income $60,000+: Standard rate

Additional Support:
- Support groups (free or low-cost)
- Crisis hotline referrals
- Resource navigation
- Community education workshops

Workshop Series (Free):
- Stress Management: 1st Tuesday monthly
- Parenting Skills: 2nd Thursday monthly
- Healthy Relationships: 3rd Wednesday monthly
