Multicultural Night

The Multicultural Night is organized by Cedar Park Elementary. This is a Cultural Celebration. Category: School.

Event Date: March 14, 2025
Time: 5:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Location: School Cafeteria and Gymnasium
Address: 789 School Lane, Cedar Park, TX 78613

Event Highlights:

Cultural Displays:
- Countries represented by student families
- Traditional clothing displays
- Arts and crafts from around the world
- Photo exhibitions
- Maps and geography displays

Performances:
- 6:00 PM: Traditional dance performances
- 6:30 PM: Musical performances from various cultures
- 7:00 PM: Student presentations
- 7:30 PM: Fashion show featuring traditional attire

Food Court:
- Sample authentic cuisines from 20+ countries
- Each family brings traditional dish to share
- Recipe cards available
- Vegetarian and allergy-friendly options labeled

Activities:
- Passport station (collect stamps from each country)
- Language learning booth
- Cultural crafts for kids
- Henna art
- Face painting
- World music zone

How to Participate:
- Represent your heritage/culture with a display table
- Perform a traditional dance or song
- Volunteer to help with setup/cleanup
- Donate food items from your culture

What to Bring:
- Traditional clothing (optional)
- Photos and artifacts
- Cultural items for display
- Food to share (serves 20-30 people)

Registration:
To Participate: Sign up to share your culture
Deadline: March 1, 2025
Volunteer Sign-up: Available online
Attendance: Free and open to all families
Call To Action: Celebrate Diversity
