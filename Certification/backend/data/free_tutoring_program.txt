Free Tutoring Program
The Free Tutoring Program is organized by Cedar Park Public Library. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the K-12 students age range.

Program Schedule:
- Fall Session: September 10, 2024 - December 20, 2024
- Spring Session: January 15, 2025 - May 30, 2025
- Summer Program: June 3, 2025 - August 15, 2025

Available Times:
- Monday-Thursday: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Location:
Cedar Park Public Library
Study Rooms and Learning Commons
550 Discovery Blvd, Cedar Park, TX 78613

Subjects Offered:

Elementary (K-5):
- Reading and literacy
- Math fundamentals
- Homework help

Middle School (6-8):
- Math (Pre-Algebra, Algebra I)
- English/Language Arts
- Science
- Social Studies
- Study skills

High School (9-12):
- Math (Algebra II, Geometry, Pre-Calculus, Calculus)
- English composition and literature
- Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
- History and Government
- SAT/ACT prep
- College application assistance

Tutors:
- Certified teachers
- College students (verified and trained)
- National Honor Society volunteers
- Retired educators

How It Works:
- Drop-in basis (no appointment needed for most subjects)
- One-on-one or small group tutoring
- Bring homework, tests, or specific questions
- Sessions typically 30-60 minutes
- Completely free of charge

Special Programs:
- SAT Prep Workshops: Monthly on Saturdays
- College Essay Writing: Fall 2024
- Summer Reading Program: June-August 2025

Registration Information:
No registration required for drop-in tutoring
SAT workshops require online registration
