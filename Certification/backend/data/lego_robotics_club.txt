Lego Robotics Club
The Lego Robotics Club is organized by STEM Education Center. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 7-14 age range.

Program Year: 2024-2025
- Fall Session: September 9, 2024 - December 13, 2024
- Spring Session: January 13, 2025 - May 9, 2025
- Summer Camp: June 2, 2025 - August 15, 2025

Meeting Schedule:

Explorers (Ages 7-9):
- Mondays, 4:00-5:30 PM
- Focus: Basic building and simple programming
- Platform: LEGO WeDo 2.0

Builders (Ages 10-12):
- Wednesdays, 4:00-5:30 PM
- Focus: Intermediate robotics and coding
- Platform: LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3

Competitors (Ages 11-14):
- Fridays, 4:00-6:00 PM
- Focus: Competition prep and advanced projects
- Platform: LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3 and Spike Prime
- Competes in FIRST LEGO League

Location:
STEM Education Center

- Mechanical engineering principles
- Basic to advanced programming

- Design thinking process
- Competition strategy
