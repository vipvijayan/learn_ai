Spanish Language Classes for Kids
The Spanish Language Classes for Kids is organized by Mundo Español Language Academy. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 4-14 age range.

Session Schedule:
- Fall Semester: September 9, 2024 - December 13, 2024
- Spring Semester: January 13, 2025 - May 16, 2025
- Summer Camp: June 2, 2025 - August 15, 2025

Age Groups:

Little Learners (Ages 4-6):
- Saturdays, 9:00-10:00 AM
- Focus: Songs, games, basic vocabulary
- Total immersion through play

Beginners (Ages 7-9):
- Tuesday/Thursday, 4:30-5:30 PM
- Focus: Conversational Spanish, reading basics
- Interactive lessons

Intermediate (Ages 10-12):
- Monday/Wednesday, 5:00-6:00 PM
- Focus: Grammar, writing, cultural studies
- Project-based learning

Advanced (Ages 13-14):
- Tuesday/Thursday, 6:00-7:00 PM
- Focus: Literature, composition, conversation
- Preparation for high school Spanish

Class Format:
- Warm-up activities and games
- Vocabulary and grammar instruction
- Conversation practice
- Cultural activities
- Songs and stories
- Homework assignments

What Students Learn:
