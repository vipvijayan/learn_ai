High School College Fair

The High School College Fair is organized by Round Rock High School. This is a College Information Fair. Category: School.

Event Date: October 3, 2024
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Location:
Venue: Round Rock High School Cafeteria
Address: 300 Lake Creek Dr, Round Rock, TX 78681

Participating Colleges & Universities:
- Over 100 colleges and universities
- Texas public universities (UT Austin, Texas A&M, Texas State, etc.)
- Private Texas schools (Baylor, SMU, Trinity, etc.)
- Out-of-state universities
- Community colleges
- Technical and trade schools
- Military academies

Fair Activities:
- College representative booths
- Financial aid workshop at 6:30 PM
- Application process seminar at 7:15 PM
- Scholarship information session at 7:45 PM
- Free SAT/ACT prep book giveaway

For Students:
- Bring questions for college reps
- Collect information and brochures
- Learn about admission requirements
- Explore degree programs
- Discuss scholarship opportunities

For Parents:
- Financial aid information
- Parent Q&A session
- Campus visit planning
- Understanding college costs
- Supporting your college-bound student

Parking:
- Available in main lot and stadium parking
- Shuttle service from overflow lot

Registration:
Cost: Free
Rsvp: Helpful but not required (helps with planning)
Call To Action: Attend and Learn
