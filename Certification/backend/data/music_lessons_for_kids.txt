Music Lessons for Kids
The Music Lessons for Kids is organized by Harmony Music Academy. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 5 and up age range.

Program Features:
This program offers several valuable features including Professional instructors, Flexible scheduling, Performance opportunities, Music theory included, Practice resources, and Recitals twice yearly.
