Music Lessons for Kids
The Music Lessons for Kids is organized by Harmony Music Academy. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 5 and up age range.

Program Year: 2024-2025
Enrollment: Year-round, rolling admissions

Instruments Offered:
- Piano (ages 5+)
- Guitar (acoustic and electric, ages 7+)
- Violin (ages 5+)
- Drums (ages 7+)
- Voice/Vocal lessons (ages 8+)
- Ukulele (ages 6+)
- Flute (ages 8+)
- Saxophone (ages 10+)
- Clarinet (ages 9+)

Lesson Formats:

Private Lessons (30, 45, or 60 minutes):
- One-on-one instruction
- Customized curriculum
- Flexible scheduling

Group Classes (60 minutes):
- 4-6 students per class
- Social learning environment
- Lower cost option

Online Lessons:
- Available for all instruments
- Same quality instruction
- Flexible from home

Class Schedule:
- Monday-Friday: 3:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Curriculum Levels:
- Beginner: No experience necessary
- Intermediate: 1-2 years experience
- Advanced: 3+ years experience
- Competition Prep: Audition preparation

Performance Opportunities:
- Winter Recital: December 10, 2024
- Spring Recital: May 18, 2025
- Talent Show: March 22, 2025
- Studio Showcases: Quarterly
- Participation in local competitions

Special Programs:
- Rock Band Program (ages 12-18)
- Music Theory Classes
- Ear Training Workshops
- Summer Music Camp: June 16-August 8, 2025

Program Features:
This program offers several valuable features including Professional instructors (all have music degrees), Flexible scheduling, Performance opportunities, Music theory included, Practice resources, Online student portal, and Recitals twice yearly.
