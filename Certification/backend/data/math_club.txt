Math Club

The Math Club is organized by Grisham Middle School. Category: School.

School Year: 2024-2025
Meeting Day: Wednesdays, 3:30 PM - 4:45 PM
Location: Room 108 (Mrs. Anderson's classroom)
First Meeting: September 11, 2024

Club Activities:

Problem Solving:
- Weekly math challenges
- Logic puzzles and brain teasers
- Real-world math applications
- Team problem-solving competitions

Math Competitions:
- MATHCOUNTS (school, chapter, state levels)
- UIL Number Sense
- American Mathematics Competition (AMC 8)
- Math Kangaroo

Fun Activities:
- Math games and strategy games
- Origami and geometric art
- Math-themed movies and videos
- Guest speakers from STEM fields
- Field trips to universities

Competition Schedule 2024-2025:
- School MATHCOUNTS: November 15, 2024
- Chapter Competition: February 8, 2025
- State MATHCOUNTS: March 15, 2025
- AMC 8: January 23, 2025
- UIL District: March 28, 2025
- UIL Regional: April 18, 2025

Monthly Themes:
- September: Introduction to competition math
- October: Number theory
- November: Geometry
- December: Logic and puzzles
- January: Algebra
- February: Probability and statistics
- March: Test prep and strategy
- April: Math in nature
- May: End-of-year celebration

Special Events:
- Pi Day Celebration: March 14, 2025
- Math Movie Night: December 13, 2024
- Parent Math Night: January 30, 2025
- End-of-Year Awards: May 21, 2025

Benefits:
- Improve problem-solving skills
- Build confidence in math
- Make friends with similar interests
- Competition experience
- College application material
- Potential scholarships

Requirements:
- Interest in mathematics (all skill levels welcome)
- Regular attendance encouraged
- Participation in at least one competition per year
- Maintain passing grades in all classes

Costs:

- Optional club T-shirt: $12
- Fundraising opportunities available
