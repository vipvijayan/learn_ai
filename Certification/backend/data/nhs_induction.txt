National Honor Society Induction

The National Honor Society Induction is organized by Anderson High School NHS. This is a Honors Ceremony. Category: School.

Event Date: October 30, 2024
Time: 7:00 PM

Location:
Venue: Anderson High School Auditorium
Address: 8403 Mesa Drive, Austin, TX 78759

Ceremony Program:
- 7:00 PM: Welcome and opening remarks
- 7:10 PM: Recognition of current NHS members
- 7:20 PM: Presentation on the Four Pillars (Scholarship, Service, Leadership, Character)
- 7:35 PM: Induction of new members
- 7:50 PM: Candlelighting ceremony
- 8:00 PM: Oath and signing of membership book
- 8:15 PM: Closing remarks and reception

New Inductees:
- Juniors and Seniors who meet NHS criteria
- Minimum 3.5 GPA
- Demonstrated leadership and service
- Outstanding character

Dress Code:
- Business formal attire
- NHS members wear NHS stole
- Inductees receive NHS pin and certificate

For Inductees:
- Arrive by 6:45 PM for lineup
- Bring completed membership forms
- Be prepared to recite NHS pledge
- Family and friends welcome to attend

Reception:
- Light refreshments in cafeteria following ceremony
- Photo opportunities
- Meet NHS officers and advisors

NHS Membership Benefits:
- National recognition on transcripts
- Scholarship opportunities
- Leadership development
- Community service projects
- Networking with peers
- College application distinction

Upcoming NHS Activities 2024-2025:
- Monthly meetings: First Wednesday of each month
- Community service projects
- Tutoring program
- School beautification
- Fundraising events
- State NHS Convention: March 2025

Requirements for Continued Membership:
- Maintain minimum 3.5 GPA
- Complete 20 service hours per semester
- Attend monthly meetings
- Participate in NHS activities
- Uphold NHS standards

Parking:
- Main student parking lot
- Visitor parking available
- Overflow at south lot

Rsvp: Inductees only (family and guests welcome, no RSVP needed)
