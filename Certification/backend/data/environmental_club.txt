Environmental Club
The Environmental Club is organized by Lake Travis High School. This program falls under the School category.

School Year: 2024-2025
Meeting Schedule: Wednesdays, 3:30-4:30 PM
Location: Science Building, Room 104
First Meeting: September 4, 2024

Club Activities:

Campus Initiatives:
- School-wide recycling program
- Campus beautification projects
- Water conservation awareness
- Energy reduction campaigns

Community Projects:
- Neighborhood clean-up days
- Tree planting events
- Creek restoration
- Community garden maintenance

Monthly Events 2024-2025:
- September: Campus Sustainability Audit
- October: Trail Clean-up at Balcones Canyonlands
- November: Composting Workshop
- December: E-Waste Collection Drive
- January: Water Quality Testing at Lake Travis
- February: Native Plant Sale Fundraiser
- March: Earth Hour Event Planning
- April: Earth Day Festival (April 22, 2025)
- May: End of Year Beach Clean-up

Special Events:
- Fall Environmental Fair: October 12, 2024
- Winter Sustainability Summit: January 24, 2025
- Earth Day Celebration: April 22, 2025

Leadership Opportunities:
- President
- Vice President
- Secretary
- Treasurer
- Project Coordinators

Benefits:
- Community service hours
- Leadership experience
- Environmental education
- College application enrichment
- Networking with local environmental organizations

Registration Information:
Registration is currently always accepting new members. Go Green with Us. No experience necessary!
