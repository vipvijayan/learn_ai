Community Garden Workshop

The Community Garden Workshop is organized by Green Austin Initiative. This is a Educational Workshop. Category: Community.

Workshop Series Dates:
- Spring Workshop 1: March 20, 2025 - Container Gardening Basics
- Spring Workshop 2: April 17, 2025 - Vegetable Garden Planning
- Spring Workshop 3: May 15, 2025 - Composting and Soil Health
- Summer Workshop: July 30, 2025 - Summer Garden Maintenance

Time: All workshops 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Location:
Venue: Community Garden Center
Address: 456 Green Way, Austin, TX 78704

Workshop Topics Include:
- Soil preparation and testing
- Plant selection for Texas climate
- Watering techniques and irrigation
- Pest management (organic methods)
- Seasonal planting schedules
- Companion planting strategies

What to Bring:
- Garden gloves
- Water bottle
- Notebook and pen
- Hat and sunscreen

Registration:
Cost: $25 per workshop or $60 for all three spring workshops
Capacity: Limited to 20 participants per workshop
Status: Open
