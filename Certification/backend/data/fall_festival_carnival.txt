Fall Festival and Carnival

The Fall Festival and Carnival is organized by Cedar Park Elementary PTA. This is a Community Festival. Category: School.

Event Date: October 26, 2024
Time: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Location:
Venue: Cedar Park Elementary School
Address: 789 School Lane, Cedar Park, TX 78613
Parking: Available in main lot and overflow at community center

Festival Activities:

Games & Attractions:
- Carnival games (ring toss, duck pond, bean bag toss)
- Bounce houses and inflatable slides
- Face painting station
- Petting zoo
- Pony rides
- Photo booth with props
- Cake walk
- Silent auction

Entertainment:
- 4:30 PM - School choir performance
- 5:30 PM - DJ and dancing
- 6:30 PM - Magic show
- 7:00 PM - Raffle drawing

Food & Refreshments:
- Hot dogs and hamburgers
- Pizza slices
- Popcorn and cotton candy
- Snow cones
- Drinks and snacks
- Bake sale items

Costs:
- Entry: Free admission
- Game Tickets: $1 each or 25 for $20
- Food: Priced $2-$5 per item
- Raffle Tickets: $5 each or 5 for $20

Raffle Prizes:
- Grand Prize: iPad
- 2nd Prize: $200 gift card to Target
- 3rd Prize: Family movie night basket
- Multiple door prizes

Volunteer Opportunities:
- Game booth operators (4:00-6:00 PM or 6:00-8:00 PM shifts)
- Food service helpers
- Set-up crew (2:00-4:00 PM)
- Clean-up crew (8:00-9:00 PM)

Registration:
Status: No registration required for attendees
Volunteer Signup: Available online at cedarparkelementarypta.org/volunteers
Call To Action: Join Us for Family Fun!
