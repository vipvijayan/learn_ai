Photography Club
The Photography Club is organized by Westwood High School. This program falls under the School category.

School Year: 2024-2025
Meeting Schedule: Tuesdays, 3:30-5:00 PM
Location: Media Lab, Room 305
First Meeting: September 10, 2024

Club Activities:

Photography Techniques:
- Composition and framing
- Lighting fundamentals
- Portrait photography
- Landscape photography
- Action and sports photography
- Macro photography
- Night photography

Digital Skills:
- Adobe Photoshop basics
- Lightroom editing
- RAW vs JPEG
- Color correction
- Photo retouching

Equipment:
- School cameras available for checkout
- DSLR and mirrorless cameras
- Various lenses
- Tripods and lighting equipment
- Backdrop and studio setup

Monthly Themes:
- September: Getting to Know Your Camera
- October: Portrait Month
- November: Nature and Landscapes
- December: Holiday Photography
- January: Black and White
- February: Love and Emotions
- March: Sports and Action
- April: Creative Concepts
- May: Year in Review

Field Trips:
- Austin Botanical Gardens: October 2024
- Downtown Austin photo walk: November 2024
- Zilker Park sunset shoot: March 2025
- Bluebonnet photography: April 2025

Competitions:
- School-wide photo contest: December 2024
- Regional high school competition: February 2025
- Texas Young Photographers Award: April 2025

Exhibitions:
- Fall Exhibition: November 15-30, 2024 (School Library)
- Spring Art Show: April 25, 2025 (School Gallery)
- Year-End Showcase: May 20, 2025 (Online and physical display)

Guest Speakers:
- Professional photographer visit: January 2025
- UT Photography department rep: March 2025
- Wedding photographer workshop: April 2025

Benefits:
Participants will gain numerous benefits such as Portfolio development, Exhibition opportunities, Discounts at local camera shop (Precision Camera 15% off), and College application enhancement.

Requirements:
- Any camera (smartphone OK for beginners)
- Interest in photography
- Willingness to participate in activities
- Attend at least 70% of meetings

Membership Fee:
- $20 per year (covers materials, printing, field trip transportation)

Registration Information:
Registration is currently open to all students. Capture the Moment. No experience necessary!
