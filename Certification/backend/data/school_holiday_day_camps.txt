School Holiday Day Camps

The School Holiday Day Camps are organized by Round Rock Parks & Recreation. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 5-12 age range.

2024-2025 Camp Schedule:

Fall Break:
- October 14-18, 2024
- 7:30 AM - 6:00 PM daily

Winter Break:
- December 23-27, 2024
- December 30, 2024 - January 3, 2025
- 7:30 AM - 6:00 PM daily
- Closed December 25 (Christmas) and January 1 (New Year's)

Spring Break:
- March 10-14, 2025
- 7:30 AM - 6:00 PM daily

Summer Camps:
- June 2 - August 15, 2025
- Weekly sessions available
- 7:30 AM - 6:00 PM daily

Location:
Round Rock Community Center
301 West Bagdad Avenue, Round Rock, TX 78664

Daily Schedule:
- 7:30-9:00 AM: Arrival and free play
- 9:00-10:00 AM: Organized activities/games
- 10:00-11:30 AM: Educational activity or field trip
- 11:30 AM-12:30 PM: Lunch
- 12:30-2:00 PM: Arts & crafts or STEM project
- 2:00-3:00 PM: Outdoor play and sports
- 3:00-4:00 PM: Snack and quiet activities
- 4:00-6:00 PM: Free play and pickup

What to Bring:
- Sack lunch and drinks (refrigeration available)
- Morning and afternoon snacks
- Sunscreen and hat
- Comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes
- Swimsuit and towel (for pool days)
- Change of clothes
- Labeled water bottle

Pricing:
- Full Day: $45 per day
- 5-Day Week: $200
- Extended care (6:00-7:30 AM or 6:00-7:00 PM): $10 per session
- Sibling discount: 10% off for 2+ children

Registration:
- Early bird discount: Register 2 weeks in advance and save 10%
- Drop-in spots available (space permitting)
- Multi-week discounts available for summer

Staff:
- Background-checked employees
