Yoga for Kids
The Yoga for Kids is organized by Peaceful Warriors Yoga Studio. This program falls under the Community category. It is designed for participants in the Ages 4-14 age range.

Benefits:
Participants will gain numerous benefits such as Flexibility and strength, Better focus and concentration, Stress relief, Body awareness, and Mindfulness skills.
