Film Club
The Film Club is organized by Round Rock High School. This program falls under the School category.

School Year: 2024-2025
Meeting Schedule: Thursdays, 3:30-5:00 PM
Location: Media Lab, Room 302
First Meeting: September 5, 2024

Club Activities:

Film Production:
- Script writing workshops
- Cinematography basics
- Video editing with Adobe Premiere
- Sound design and mixing
- Lighting techniques

Projects:
- Short film competitions
- Documentary projects
- Music video productions
- Public service announcements
- Film festival submissions

Annual Events:
- Fall Film Fest: November 15, 2024 (screening of member films)
- Winter Documentary Showcase: February 7, 2025
- Spring Film Festival: April 25, 2025 (competitive event)
- Year-End Awards Night: May 22, 2025

Equipment Access:
- DSLR cameras and lenses
- Microphones and audio recorders
- Lighting kits
- Tripods and stabilizers
- Editing computers with software

Competition Opportunities:
- Texas Student Film Festival: March 2025
- National High School Film Festival: April 2025
- Local 48 Hour Film Challenge: October 2024

Guest Speakers:
- October: Local film director
- January: Austin Film Society representative
- March: UT Film School professor

Benefits:
- Hands-on filmmaking experience
- Portfolio development
- College application material
- Networking with film professionals
- Potential scholarship opportunities

Registration Information:
Registration is currently join anytime during the school year. Start Creating Films. No experience necessary!
