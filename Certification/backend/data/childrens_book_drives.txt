Children's Book Drives
The Children's Book Drives is organized by Community Literacy Foundation. This program falls under the School category.

2024-2025 Collection Schedule:
- Fall Drive: October 1-31, 2024
- Winter Drive: December 1-31, 2024
- Spring Drive: March 1-31, 2025
- Summer Drive: June 1-30, 2025

Collection Locations:
- All Round Rock ISD Elementary Schools
- Public Libraries in Round Rock and Cedar Park
- Community Centers
- Local bookstores and retail partners

Books Needed:
- Picture books for ages 0-5
- Early readers for ages 6-8
- Chapter books for ages 9-12
- Young adult fiction for ages 13-17
- Educational and STEM books

Program Features:
This program offers several valuable features including Year-round collection, Tax-deductible donations, Community impact reports, and Reading incentive programs.

2024 Impact:
- Over 15,000 books collected
- 2,500 children served
- 12 school libraries restocked
