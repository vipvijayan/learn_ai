Girl Scouts Troop Recruitment
The Girl Scouts Troop Recruitment is organized by Girl Scouts of Central Texas. This program falls under the Community category. 

Meeting Schedule:
- Frequency: Twice monthly
- Typical_Day: Varies by troop
- Duration: 1.5-2 hours

Registration is currently year-round enrollment. Join Girl Scouts
