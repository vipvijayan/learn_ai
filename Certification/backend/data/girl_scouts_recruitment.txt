Girl Scouts Troop Recruitment
The Girl Scouts Troop Recruitment is organized by Girl Scouts of Central Texas. This program falls under the Community category.

Membership Year: 2024-2025
Open Enrollment: Year-round

Girl Scout Levels:

Daisy (Kindergarten - 1st Grade):
- Meets twice monthly
- Focus on making friends and trying new things
- Typical meeting day: Monday or Tuesday afternoons

Brownie (2nd - 3rd Grade):
- Meets twice monthly
- Focus on discovering new hobbies and helping community
- Typical meeting day: Wednesday afternoons

Junior (4th - 5th Grade):
- Meets twice monthly
- Focus on making a difference and earning badges
- Typical meeting day: Thursday afternoons

Cadette (6th - 8th Grade):
- Meets twice monthly
- Focus on leadership and adventure
- Typical meeting day: Thursday evenings

Senior (9th - 10th Grade):
- Meets twice monthly
- Focus on bigger projects and global connections
- Typical meeting day: Varies by troop

Ambassador (11th - 12th Grade):
- Meets twice monthly
- Focus on changing the world and college prep
- Typical meeting day: Varies by troop

Meeting Schedule:
- Frequency: Twice monthly (some troops meet weekly)
- Duration: 1.5-2 hours
- Location: Schools, community centers, or churches

Activities Include:
- Badge earning and skill building
- Outdoor adventures and camping
- Community service projects
- STEM activities
- Arts and crafts
- Cookie sales program (January-March 2025)
- Field trips and special events

Upcoming Events 2024-2025:
- Fall Product Sale: September-October 2024
- Cookie Season: January-March 2025
- Summer Camp: June-August 2025
- STEM Day: November 2, 2024
- World Thinking Day: February 22, 2025

Costs:
- Annual Membership: $25
- Troop dues: Varies by troop (typically $10-20/month)
- Uniforms and materials: Optional
- Financial assistance available

Benefits:
- Lifelong friendships
- Leadership skills
- Confidence building
- College scholarships available
- Resume/college application enrichment

Registration Information:
Registration is currently year-round enrollment. Join Girl Scouts. Find your local troop at gsctx.org/find-a-troop
